Why SEA

The KEY advantage to SEA model is that everything we teach in the classroom our students are able, encouraged and required to execute in real life.

We have GLOBAL network of strategic partners and funding sources.

Our faculty are EXPERTS in every vertical and subject with in-depth network of all stakeholders in a particular industry.

SEA goal is not to give you a DIPLOMA (each graduate get one)but real life experience and knowledge, so you and your team can be successful in real market place.

Mission

Our mission is to give individuals with perseverance, ambition, and initiative a way to grow their business and execute their ideas. We provide them with an educational opportunity based on real life experiences that leads to self-discovery, pursuit of knowledge and ability to adapt to ever changing social and economic environments.

Founder

Experience may be a great teacher, the combination of experience and education is unsurpassed in improving the odds for entrepreneurial success.”

Henry Shterenberg,
Founder of Social Entrepreneurship Academy

Every year, approximately 600,000 new ventures are created in the U.S. and at any given time, as many as 1 in 10 American adults are trying to start a new business. Less than one half of these ventures will still be operating after three years. However, studies have shown that there are practices such as developing and executing a professional business plan which can significantly increase one’s chance for success.

I urge everyone to get involved in this program. Regardless of your role – apprenticeship provider, mentor, faculty, sponsor, guest lecturer or investor, you will find a great deal of personal satisfaction and growth.
